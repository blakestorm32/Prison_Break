PRISON BREAK – REAL-TIME SOCIAL DEDUCTION GAME
=============================================

GAME OVERVIEW
-------------
A real-time, 2D top-down, tile-based multiplayer social deduction game set in a high-security prison.

Players move and act simultaneously in real time.
Hidden roles, asymmetric win conditions, spatial control, and multiple escape paths drive deception.

The game progresses through real-time DAY / NIGHT cycles.
The game emphasizes movement, access permissions, items, and information control.

Target player count: 5–12 players.


CORE GAME FLOW
--------------
- The game runs continuously in real time.
- The game alternates between DAY and NIGHT phases.
- Certain abilities, cards, and systems reset or trigger on phase changes.

The game ends immediately when ANY global end condition is met.


GLOBAL END CONDITIONS
---------------------
- 6 day/night cycles complete
- Warden dies
- Gang Leader escapes
- All gang members die


ROLES
-----

AUTHORITIES
-----------
Warden
- Alignment: Good OR Evil (random per game)
- Hearts: 5
- Bullets: 3
- Baton: Yes
- Win Conditions:
  - Good Warden: No prisoners escape
  - Evil Warden: Gang Leader escapes
- Special Ability:
  - Alarm (once per day):
      - Triggers prison-wide alarm remotely
      - Alarm lasts 5 seconds
      - If no prisoners are in restricted areas, alarm auto-stops
      - If prisoners are in restricted areas:
          - NPC prison guards begin shooting them

Deputy
- Alignment: Good OR Evil
- Hearts: 4
- Bullets: 3
- Baton: Yes
- Win Conditions (rotates per game):
  - Detective: Kill the Gang Leader
  - Guard: No one escapes after 10 day/night cycles
  - Evil Deputy: Gang Leader escapes
- Base Ability:
  - Search (once per day):
      - Search EITHER a player OR a cell
      - Confiscate contraband found


PRISONERS
---------
Gang Leader
- Hearts: 3
- Win Conditions:
  - Escape the prison
  - Kill the Warden
- Special:
  - Chooses Black Market location each NIGHT
  - May privately tell chosen players the location

Gang Member
- Hearts: 3
- Win Conditions:
  - Gang Leader escapes
  - Warden is killed
- Succession Rule:
  - If Gang Leader dies:
      - One surviving Gang Member becomes the new Gang Leader
      - If multiple exist, selection is random

Snitch (Good Prisoner)
- Hearts: 3
- Win Condition:
  - Gang Leader is killed
  - OR no gang members escape by end of game

Neutral Prisoner
- Hearts: 3
- Alignment: Neutral
- Win Conditions (rotates per game):
  - Hitman: Assigned a random target; must personally kill them
  - Escape Artist: Escape before anyone else

BOARD / MAP
-----------
2D tile grid with rooms and corridors.

Rooms:
- Prison Cells (1 per player)
- Warden’s HQ
- Camera Room
- Cafeteria
- Courtyard
- Power Room
- Ammunition Room
- Mail Room
- Roof Lookout
- Black Market (rotates nightly)

Corridors connect all rooms.


ACCESS RULES
------------
Cells:
- Each player has one assigned cell
- Only cell owner or Authorities can open/close cell door
- Any player may be locked inside any cell

Warden HQ:
- Only Warden may enter

Camera Room:
- Authorities only
- Disabled if power is off

Black Market:
- Prisoners only
- Location set nightly by Gang Leader


POWER SYSTEM
------------
Power ON:
- Cameras active
- Ammo room accessible
- Doors open/close normally

Power OFF:
- Cameras disabled
- Ammo room locked
- Doors open but cannot be closed


COMBAT & PENALTIES
------------------
Weapons:
- Baton (Authorities):
    - No damage
    - Knocks player back
    - Stuns for 3 seconds
    - Cooldown applies
    - No penalty for use

- Shiv:
    - Deals 0.5 heart damage
    - Silent

- Guns:
    - Require bullets
    - Standard damage: 1 heart

- Golden Bullet:
    - One per game
    - Deals 2 hearts damage

Authority Shooting Penalty:
- If Warden or Deputy shoots a player:
    - Locked in quarters for rest of DAY
EXCEPT IF:
    - Target is in restricted area
    - Power is off
    - Target is holding illegal contraband
      (gun, shiv, ladder, shovel, wire cutters)


ESCAPE ROUTES
-------------
- Courtyard Dig (shovel)
- Badge Escape
- Power-Out Escape
- Ladder Escape (requires 2 ladders)
- Roof / Helicopter Escape (requires keys)

ABILITY BEHAVIOR OVERVIEW
-------------------------
Authority abilities
- Camera Man:
  - Use in Camera Room while power is ON
  - Opens camera-view mode for a limited duration
  - Can cycle one room at a time
- Search:
  - Search one player per day
  - Shows a report of target inventory and confiscates contraband
- Detainer:
  - Can detain a prisoner for 15 seconds
  - Max uses per day: 2
- Tracker:
  - Activates tracker-view mode for 30 seconds
  - Shows recent footsteps per player in that player's color

Prisoner abilities
- Pick Pocket:
  - Steal one random item from a target player
  - Max uses per day: 1
- Hacker:
  - Cuts prison power (disables camera systems)
- Disguise:
  - Become disguised as NPC for 20 seconds
- Locksmith:
  - Unlock/open one valid door for prisoners
  - Max uses per day: 1
- Chameleon:
  - Stand still for 5 seconds to turn invisible
  - Invisibility remains until movement


PRISONER MONEY TASKS (NPC INTERACTION)
--------------------------------------
- Prisoners earn money through NPC prisoner tasks during DAY.
- Interact with an NPC prisoner to receive a task.
- Task types:
  - Visit-room task: go to the requested room, then report back to an NPC prisoner.
  - Hold-item task: carry the requested item, then report back to an NPC prisoner.
- Completing a task grants Money card rewards.
- Money cards are then spent at night in the Black Market.


CARDS SYSTEM
------------
- Players may store up to 3 cards
- Cards are one-time use
- Cards may be used at any time unless stated otherwise


CARDS
-----
Morphine
- Restore 1 heart

Bullet
- Gain +1 bullet

Money
- Usable in Black Market
- Can bribe NPC prisoners

Speed
- Temporary movement speed boost

Armor Plate
- Next round, gain +1 temporary heart

Lock Snap
- Next round, break one lock
- Lock repaired after round

Item Steal
- Choose a player and steal a specific item

Item Grab
- Choose a player and gain a random item

Scrap Bundle
- Gain crafting material

Door Stop
- Lock a door for 10 seconds

Get Out of Jail Free
- Prisoner: removes solitary confinement
- Authority: removes penalty for unjust shooting


DESIGN PRINCIPLES
-----------------
- Real-time play
- No voting mechanics
- Abilities are faction-based
- Cards are consumable, tactical, and goal-agnostic
- Multiple overlapping win conditions
- Spatial control replaces social voting
